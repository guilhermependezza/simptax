Checklist

Year-End Tax Prep

Documents to Gather

  • W-2s from all employers
  • 1099s (NEC, MISC, INT, DIV, B)
  • Mortgage interest (Form 1098)
  • Student loan interest (1098-E)
  • Tuition payments (1098-T)
  • Health insurance (1095-A/B/C)
  • Property tax statements
  • Charitable donation receipts
  • Medical expense receipts
  • Business expense receipts

Before Filing

  • Maximize retirement contributions (IRA deadline: April 15)
  • Review for errors on all forms
  • Check prior year carryovers
  • Consider itemizing vs standard deduction
  • File or request extension by April 15
